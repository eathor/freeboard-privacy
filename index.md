# Privacy Policy — Vineyard Observations

_Last updated: April 15, 2026_

**Vineyard Observations** is an internal tool used by Freeboard Asset Management staff to record vineyard field observations.

## Information We Collect

The app collects the following data, submitted by authorized users during their work activities:

- **Voice recordings** of spoken field observations
- **Photos** taken of vineyards, blocks, and vine conditions
- **GPS location** used to identify the nearest vineyard block when recording an observation
- **Text notes** typed by the user
- **Email address** used for account authentication

## How We Use Your Information

Data collected through the app is used solely for internal vineyard management, crop monitoring, and operational decision-making at Freeboard Asset Management. Data is stored in a private, access-controlled database and is only visible to authorized Freeboard staff.

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, rent, or share user data with third parties. Data is not used for advertising. Voice recordings may be processed by automated transcription and analysis services (OpenAI Whisper and Anthropic Claude) strictly for the purpose of converting speech to structured observation records.

## Data Retention

Observation data is retained indefinitely for historical analysis. Users may request deletion of their submissions by contacting the address below.

## Your Rights

Users can request access to, correction of, or deletion of their personal data by contacting us.
